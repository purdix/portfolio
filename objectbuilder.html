Introduction

What is Rose Rocket?

Rose Rocket is a cloud-based Transportation Management System (TMS) built for trucking companies and brokerages. It provides essential tools for managing daily operations, including dispatching, customer communication, billing, and driver management.

The Problem

In the trucking industry, no two companies are the same. Each one operates with incredibly diverse internal processes and needs. Yet, most TMS solutions provide rigid, one-size-fits-all systems that force companies to conform to the software. For years, Rose Rocket followed a similar approach, leading to several critical challenges:

Limited customization*

Rose Rocket often failed to capture key details unique to each customer’s business, limiting their ability to tailor their system.

Inefficient workarounds*

Our Solutions team often resorted to complex workarounds to meet customer needs, introducing frustrating inefficiencies into their workflows.

Lack of self-service*

Customers struggled to set up their organizations independently, resulting in high support demands and time-consuming configurations.

High development costs

Customizing the product for individual needs demanded substantial development resources, leading to costly and unsustainable roadmaps.

* Based on over 100+ open customer support tickets from our previous software

The Objective

Faced with these challenges, our goal was clear:

Empower users to tailor the software to their unique needs by enabling deep customization of core functionality

Our Approach

To achieve this objective, we aimed to tackle the majority of common challenges (80%) with standard, out-of-the-box features, while addressing the more specialized needs (20%) with builder-esque tools.

The first of these builders, named "Object Builder", aims to solve for these specialized needs by enabling businesses to define and customize Objects according to their specific requirements.

What are "Objects"?

Objects are types of information stored in Rose Rocket, such as Orders, Quotes, Tasks, Customers, and Invoices. Each Object has associated fields that appear as columns on a board or as rows in the data panel.

Key Metrics

To track success, we focused on two key metrics:

PRIMARY METRIC

Increase % of SQLs*

Raise the percentage of leads qualified early in the sales pipeline by improving product fit (via Object Builder customization).

*Sales Qualified Leads

SECONDARY METRIC

Increase adoption rate

Increase # of unique object configurations per organization, reflecting greater engagement with customization features.

Phase 1: Proof of Concept

Our Goal

Object Builder was initially launched to our internal Sales and Solutions teams with a limited set of features, including:

  • Adding new custom fields
  • Creating new sections
  • Reorganizing fields

The goal was for them to start demoing the builder in sales calls, demonstrating its potential to prospective customers, and helping us evaluate its functionality and adoption.
